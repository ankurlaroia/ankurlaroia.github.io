About Ankur Laroia

Passionate Data Scientist and Machine Learning Engineer

Greetings,

I'm Ankur Laroia, a passionate Data Scientist and Machine Learning Engineer driven by the pursuit of leveraging data to fuel innovation and drive strategic decision-making. With a solid educational background, including a Master's degree in Business Analytics Data Science Cohort from The University of Texas at Dallas and a Bachelor's in Computer Science Engineering from SRM Institute of Science and Technology, I possess a robust blend of theoretical knowledge and practical expertise.

Certified as an AWS Cloud Practitioner, I pride myself on my ability to communicate effectively, provide exceptional customer service, and make informed decisions using an analytical approach. My technical skills encompass a wide range of programming languages, including R, Python, and SQL, along with proficiency in machine learning libraries such as TensorFlow and scikit-learn, and data visualization tools like Tableau and PowerBI.

My project portfolio reflects my commitment to delivering impactful results. From developing sophisticated machine learning models for credit risk modeling to spearheading initiatives in diabetic retinopathy detection, I've consistently demonstrated my ability to preprocess data, engineer features, and deploy models effectively. My professional experience as a Data Science Intern at Wolters Kluwer and Programmer Analyst at Cognizant Technology Solutions has honed my ability to drive actionable insights and facilitate tangible improvements in business performance.

I'm passionate about exploring opportunities to collaborate on exciting projects and make a meaningful impact in the field of data science and machine learning. Let's connect on LinkedIn and embark on a journey of transformative data-driven decision-making.

Best regards,
Ankur Laroia
